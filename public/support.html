‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my payment handle?
Go to your Profile page in the app and update your Venmo, Square, or PayPal handle. Make sure it's correct before the week ends if you're winning!
When are weekly winners announced?
Winners are announced every Monday. The Hot Take with the highest average Heat Score from the previous week wins the jackpot. You'll receive a notification if you win!
How do I receive my prize?
Prizes are sent to your linked payment handle (Venmo, Square, or PayPal) within 5 business days of the winner announcement. Make sure your payment handle is correct and active.
Can I see my past Hot Takes?
Currently, Hot Takes are anonymous once submitted. You won't see a list of your past Takes in the app. This keeps the focus on the community ratings rather than individual tracking.
How is the Heat Score calculated?
The Heat Score is the average of all ratings (0-100 on the Scoville scale) that your Hot Take receives. The more people rate your Take, the more stable the average becomes.

What happens if I violate the Terms of Service?
