Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Hot Takes! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - For account creation and authentication
  • Display name - Your chosen username visible to other users
  • Password - Stored securely and encrypted
  • Social login credentials - If you choose to sign in with Google or Facebook
  • Payment handle - Your Venmo, Square, or PayPal username to process prize payouts

1.2 Content You Create

We collect and store:

  • Hot Takes - The text of opinions you submit to the platform
  • Ratings - Your ratings of other users' Hot Takes (0-100 on the Scoville scale)
  • Avatar selection - Your chosen jalapeño pepper icon
  • Timestamps - When you create Takes or submit ratings

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage statistics (features used, time spent in app)
  • Performance data and crash reports
  • IP address and general location data (city/country level only)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide the Service - Display Hot Takes, process ratings, calculate Heat Scores
  • Manage Weekly Competitions - Determine winners and process prize payouts
  • Improve User Experience - Optimize the feed algorithm to show you relevant content
  • Ensure Fair Play - Detect and prevent abuse, spam, or violation of Terms of Service
  • Communicate - Send notifications about weekly winners, app updates, or account changes
  • Display Ads - Show relevant advertisements to support the free service
  • Analytics - Understand how users engage with the app to improve features

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

3.1 With Other Users

  • Your display name and avatar are visible to all users
  • Your Hot Takes are public and anonymous (not directly linked to your profile)
  • Weekly winners' display names appear on the Hall of Flame

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting - Firebase/Google Cloud Platform for data storage
  • Payment processors - To facilitate prize payouts via Venmo, Square, or PayPal
  • Analytics services - To understand app performance and usage
  • Ad networks - To display advertisements in the app
  • Authentication providers - Google and Facebook for social login

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Protect user safety

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction - Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out - Disable push notifications or marketing communications
  • Data portability - Export your data in a common format

To exercise these rights, contact us at jbuxofplenty@gmail.com or visit our Support page.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Personal information (email, payment handle) is deleted within 30 days
  • Hot Takes may remain visible but are anonymized (no link to your account)
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days

7. Children's Privacy

Hot Takes is not intended for users under 13 years old (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Advertising and Analytics

Hot Takes displays ads to support the free service. Our ad partners may collect:

  • Device identifiers and ad IDs
  • General location data
  • App usage patterns

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (iOS: Limit Ad Tracking, Android: Opt out of Ads Personalization).

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. We use secure infrastructure and comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for EU users.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of Hot Takes after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: jbuxofplenty@gmail.com
  • Support Page: hottakes.app/support

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access, correct, or delete your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
