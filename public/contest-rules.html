Official Contest Rules

Last Updated: November 2025

These Official Contest Rules ("Rules") govern participation in all contests (each, a "Contest") operated by Hot Takes ("Company," "we," or "us") within the Hot Takes mobile or web application ("App"). By creating an account or submitting content in the App, you ("you," "user," or "participant") agree to these Rules and our Privacy Policy.

1. Eligibility

  • Open only to legal residents of the United States who are 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.
  • Void where prohibited by law.
  • Employees, contractors, and affiliates of the Company (and their immediate family members) are not eligible to win.
  • Participation constitutes full and unconditional agreement to these Rules and to the Company's decisions, which are final and binding.

2. How to Enter

  • No purchase or payment is necessary to enter or win.
  • To participate, users must create an account within the App and post a qualifying comment during the active Contest period.
  • Comments must comply with all content rules described in Section 7 below.

3. Contest Periods

  • Contests run weekly, beginning at 12:00 a.m. Eastern Time each Monday and ending at 11:59 p.m. Eastern Time each Sunday.
  • All timestamps are based on U.S. Eastern Time unless otherwise noted.

4. How Winners Are Determined

  • Each week, the eligible comment that receives the highest overall user rating (as determined by in-app voting) will be deemed the winner.
  • In the event of a tie, the Company reserves the right to:
    • Use a neutral tiebreaker method such as random selection among the tied entries; or
    • Allow multiple winners to share the prize.
  • Company reserves the right to disqualify votes suspected to be fraudulent, automated, or manipulated.

5. Prizes

  • The weekly winner will receive a cash prize in the amount announced within the App prior to or during that week's Contest.
  • Prizes are non-transferable and no substitutions are permitted, except at the Company's discretion.
  • Winners will be notified via the App or by email within seven (7) days after the Contest ends.
  • Payments will be made via PayPal, Venmo, Stripe, or another digital payment service as determined by the Company.

6. Taxes

  • All federal, state, and local taxes are the sole responsibility of the winner.
  • Winners who receive $600 or more in total prizes in a calendar year may be required to complete a Form W-9 and will receive an IRS Form 1099 for tax reporting purposes.

7. Content Rules

By submitting a comment or other content, you agree that your submission:

  • Must be your original work and not infringe on any third-party rights.
  • Must not contain hate speech, harassment, illegal activity, spam, or misleading information.
  • Must not include copyrighted material, trademarks, or personal information of others without permission.
  • Must comply with the App's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.

The Company reserves the right to remove any comment and disqualify any participant for violation of these rules or for any behavior deemed inconsistent with the spirit of the Contest.

8. Winner Verification

  • Potential winners are subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with these Rules.
  • Failure to respond to notification within seven (7) days or failure to complete verification requirements may result in disqualification and selection of an alternate winner.

9. Rights Granted by Participants

By submitting a comment, you grant the Company a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, display, reproduce, and distribute your submission for promotional or operational purposes related to the Contest and the App.

10. Limitation of Liability

By participating, you agree that the Company and its affiliates, officers, employees, and agents are not responsible for:

  • Lost, late, misdirected, or failed communications;
  • Technical errors or interruptions in service;
  • Any injury, loss, or damage resulting from participation or acceptance of a prize.

The Company's total liability for any claim arising out of or related to this Contest shall not exceed the total prize value awarded in the applicable Contest.

11. General Conditions

  • The Company reserves the right to cancel, modify, or suspend any Contest at any time for any reason, including technical issues or suspected fraud.
  • These Rules are governed by the laws of the United States, without regard to its conflict-of-law provisions.
  • Any disputes shall be resolved exclusively in the appropriate state or federal courts.
