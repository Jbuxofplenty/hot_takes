- Open only to legal residents of the United States who are **18 years of age or older** at the time of entry.

## 2. How to Enter

- No purchase or payment is necessary to enter or win.
- To participate, users must create an account within the App and post a qualifying comment during the active Contest period.
- Comments must comply with all content rules described in Section 7 below.

## 3. Contest Periods

- Contests run **weekly**, beginning at 12:00 a.m. Eastern Time each Monday and ending at 11:59 p.m. Eastern Time each Sunday.
- All timestamps are based on U.S. Eastern Time unless otherwise noted.

## 4. How Winners Are Determined

- Each week, the eligible comment that receives the **highest overall user rating** (as determined by in-app voting) will be deemed the winner.
- In the event of a tie, the Company reserves the right to:
  - Use a neutral tiebreaker method such as random selection among the tied entries; or
  - Allow multiple winners to share the prize.
- Company reserves the right to disqualify votes suspected to be fraudulent, automated, or manipulated.

- Prizes are non-transferable and no substitutions are permitted, except at the Company’s discretion.
- Winners will be notified via the App or by email within seven (7) days after the Contest ends.
- Payments will be made via PayPal, Venmo, Stripe, or another digital payment service as determined by the Company.
